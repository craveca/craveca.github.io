How to Activate Your Crave Account

To activate your Crave account, follow these simple steps:

  1. Visit the Crave website or open the Crave app on your device.
  2. Click on the "Sign In" button located at the top right corner of the page.
  3. Enter your email address and TV code in the respective fields.
  4. Click on the "Activate Now" button.
  5. Follow any additional on-screen instructions to complete the activation process.

If you encounter any issues during the activation process, please contact Crave support for assistance.
